Frequently asked questions

What is Magnisys Technology's payment policy?

We accept all major credit cards, Payoneer, PayPal, and bank wire transfers as payment methods.

How are you continuing to run your business despite the COVID-19 pandemic's difficult circumstances?

Magnisys Technology employees are all working from their homes and at a safe distance from COVID-19. We are only a phone call away from one another in terms of business continuity (Google Meet, Zoom, or Skype). Additionally, we use high-end software tools for real-time work collaboration that give the impression that we are all working together in the same location. We haven't had a single snag in our efforts thus far.

How will I be able to manage a remote team if I've never done it before?

A virtual office will be installed and set up for you with the help of Magnisys Technology. This will assist you in project management, communication (through text, file sharing, and video call), and progress monitoring. You may always reach us by clicking or tapping on a screen. You are in command at all times.

What type of support and upkeep services would Magnisys Technology provide for me?

Magnisys Technology is renowned for its unwavering maintenance and customer service offerings. We provide routine software inspections and debugging services to uphold such requirements. To guarantee faultless execution of your product, we provide a variety of services, including software migration, 3rd-Party API creation, and more.

For my project, are you willing to sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA)?

Absolutely! Our top focus is maintaining your anonymity. When discussing the project, you can talk to our experts about the terms of our NDA. We make sure every clause and procedure is as clear as possible for you. Our comprehensive client experience includes more than simply our top-notch software development. Some of our areas of expertise include professionalism, assistance, communication, and confidentiality.

In what capacities can I count on you for my development project?

Complete software and mobile app development is a specialty of Magnisys Technology. Therefore, you can anticipate all roles during your development project, from business analyst to developers, from designers to delivery manager. Additionally, teams can be customised to meet your needs.

Can you help? I'm not sure what kind of services I need for my development project?

Simply get in touch with Magnisys Technology's experts. I'm done now. The rest will be handled by us. At every stage of the project, we will work with you in complete openness.

How can I hire dedicated developers from Magnisys Technology?

Contact our specialists, go through the specifics of your project, then set up the interviews. Organise your chosen pros into a team. then decide which hiring strategy you like. And that's it. The project will thereafter be handled by us.

Tell me about the way your project is carried out?

Everything begins with an idea; during this phase, we go through the project's requirements, review the work's scope and target audience, assess the technologies to be used, determine which features are necessary, etc. Wireframe design and graphic design are the first steps in the project when the paperwork is confirmed. To create software of the highest calibre, we use the Agile Methodology throughout development.

What level of experience on average do Magnisys Technology developers have?

Magnisys Technology is a business with a solid track record spanning more than ten years. Additionally, it is a very welcoming environment where success, hard work, and talent are celebrated. As a result, developers with expertise spanning from 3 to 12 years are available.

Will I have access to project management tools and timesheet reports?

Absolutely! Your project will always be in your control. As a result, you will have full access to project management tools and timesheet data.

What kind of development process is employed at Magnisys Technology?

Magnisys Technology uses the Agile Methodology to provide the best possible software development.

What channels of communication are employed at Magnisys Technology?

Magnisys Technology uses almost all of the commonly used communication tools and ways to ensure constant contact. Phone conversations, email, video calls (through Skype, Google Meet, Facebook Messenger, and others), Slack, Microsoft Teams, and WhatsApp are just a few examples.

Who will own the source code?

None but you. You will be the sole owner of all intellectual property, including the code. Our specialists can be consulted for more information. We are always pleased to assist.
